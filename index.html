Our Story
The Faces Behind TechFix 👋

We're just two friends from KL who are genuinely obsessed with fixing things. No big corporate nonsense — just skill, honesty, and a lot of coffee.

How It All Started

TechFix KL started back in 2022 when Haziq and Luqman got tired of seeing friends overpay for simple laptop repairs at big shopping mall shops. Armed with technical skills and a tiny rented space in Dato' Keramat, they opened their doors — and never looked back. What started as word-of-mouth has grown into one of the most trusted repair spots in the neighbourhood.

🧑‍🔧 Co-Founder
Muhammad Haziq
Age 27

Haziq is the hardware wizard of the duo. With a Diploma in Electronics Engineering from Politeknik Sultan Mizan Zainal Abidin and 5+ years of hands-on experience, he can diagnose a faulty motherboard just by looking at it. He handles screen replacements, motherboard repairs, and water damage recovery like second nature.

Hardware Repair Motherboard Screen Replacement Water Damage
👨‍💻 Co-Founder
Ahmad Luqman
Age 25

Luqman is the software guy. A self-taught developer and IT enthusiast, he specialises in virus removal, OS reinstallation, data recovery, and performance tuning. He's the one customers call when their laptop is "acting slow" or they've accidentally deleted everything — he's gotten pretty good at saving the day.
